# Anti-Detection Rules — Social Media Automation

**Read this BEFORE any social media automation session.**

## Rate Limiting

1. **Random delay** between EVERY action: 0.5-2 seconds
2. **Random delay** between skill steps: 20-90 seconds
3. **Max session:** 45 minutes, then 10 min break

## Behavior Mixing

4. **Mix activities** — don't just outreach. Like, scroll feed, watch stories, read posts
5. **Never send identical messages** — each must be unique, personalized
6. **Vary timing** — don't always act at the exact same second intervals

## Account Safety

7. **Warm-up new account:** 3 days of normal manual use before any automation
8. **Weekend:** reduce activity to 50%
9. **Skip random sessions:** 1 session per day gets randomly skipped
10. **Daily limits per platform:**
    - LinkedIn: ~30 connection requests, ~50 likes, ~20 comments
    - Instagram: ~40 likes, ~15 DMs, ~10 follows
    - (Adjust based on account age and standing)

## Red Flags to Avoid

- Rapid-fire identical actions (10 likes in 10 seconds)
- Copy-paste DM messages
- Following/unfollowing same people repeatedly
- Operating 24/7 without breaks
- Sudden spike in activity from a dormant account
